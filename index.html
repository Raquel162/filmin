Filmin

Bringing independent films to the general public

Comunidad Filmin S.L., doing business as Filmin, is a Spanish subscription video on demand streaming service, primarily dedicated to streaming independent films, as well as a production company and film distributor. The company headquarters is located in Barcelona.

The platform has more than 9,000 titles, including films, mostly independent, series and shorts. The content is categorized by Film Genre (Animation, Drama, Comedy, Thriller, ...), tags and, what the platform calls, Moods (What do you feel like watching now...).
